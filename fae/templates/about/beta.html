Testing Guidelines

During the beta testing phase, it's important to note the following:

  • As new beta versions of FAE are made available leading up to the release of 1.0, rules will change — new rules will be added, and existing rules may be modified. This means that reports run with a previous beta version may not display properly in the current or subsequent versions.
  • To avoid incompatibilities as rules are added and modified, the reports database will be purged when new beta versions are released. This will include reports that are marked for permanent archiving.
